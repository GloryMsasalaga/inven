Our values, mission & vision

Core principle

Our Values

We believe in customer service and customer satisfaction, and the need to continuously perfect the same. Every solution we build is rooted in integrity, transparency, and a commitment to excellence that never wavers.

Direction

Our Mission

Inventions Technologies shall design, implement and maintain VoIP infrastructure and establish new contact centres for a range of clients from SMEs to corporate clients — using technology which allows the consolidation of both voice and data packets. We help clients expand and exceed their customer sales, services and marketing expectations.

Purpose

Our Vision

To be the most reliable VoIP company in Tanzania and have the largest satisfied clientele of any VoIP company in the country — building a connected nation, one business at a time.

Philosophy

Our Belief

We work hard to provide tools that cut expenses and increase profit. Our commitment to scalable, adaptable and customer-friendly solutions — backed by strategic UC partnerships — never fails.
